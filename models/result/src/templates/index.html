Naive Bayes Classifier

Uses the Multinomial Naive Bayes algorithm, ideal for text classification. It calculates the probability of an email being spam based on word frequencies.

📝

TF-IDF Vectorization

Converts email text into numerical feature vectors using Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency, capturing the importance of each word.

💫

Text Preprocessing

Cleans emails by removing punctuation, numbers, URLs, and special characters, then converts to lowercase for consistent analysis.
